Step by Step Tutorial

Begin by placing your camera that can capture the side of your body during exercises. Connect it to your computer or smartphone.

Download and open the AI workout app on your device. Ensure your camera is connected and calibrated.

Choose the exercise you want to perform from the app's exercise library. Make sure to select the correct form you want to follow

Start your workout while the AI camera records your movements. Follow the on-screen instructions for each exercise.

After each repetition, the AI camera will analyze your form and provide feedback on your screen. Pay attention to the feedback and make necessary adjustments.

Continue with your workout, making improvements based on the AI's feedback. The more you practice, the better your form will become.

With consistent practice and AI guidance, you can perfect your workout form and minimize the risk of injuries.
